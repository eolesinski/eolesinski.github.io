Using novel, experimental data provdied by the U.S. Census Bureau, we analyzed community resilience to external shocks (COVID-19) at regional, state, county, and census tract level granularities. Analysis was presented to senior cabinet officials in (D) Governor Sisolak's Administration to be quoted in public remarks regarding economic diversificaiton.
